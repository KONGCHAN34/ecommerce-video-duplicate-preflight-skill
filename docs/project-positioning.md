# Project Positioning

## One-Sentence Positioning

Ecommerce Video Duplicate Preflight Skill is a Codex Skill for content, editing, and e-commerce teams to scan local video folders, identify exact or likely duplicate assets, and generate publishing-priority reports using safe read-only workflows.

## Problem

Teams often have many product videos, edited variants, re-exports, and historical assets. Platforms may evaluate repeated or highly similar material, but manual preflight review is slow, inconsistent, and difficult to audit.

## Users

- E-commerce content teams.
- Product video editors.
- Short-video operators.
- Performance marketing creative teams.
- Small merchants and brands.
- Agent users building local media QA workflows.

## Core Scenarios

- Publishing preflight: choose lower duplicate-risk videos first.
- Editing review: decide whether a new version is meaningfully different.
- Batch cleanup: identify exact duplicates and review candidate material families.
- Report handoff: give non-technical teammates a review queue.

## Non-Goals

- This is not a platform approval guarantee.
- This is not a copyright ownership checker.
- This is not a face recognition or identity tool.
- This is not an automatic delete, move, upload, or publishing workflow.
- This is not a prompt collection with no executable workflow.

## Public Claim Boundary

The project may claim:

- Local read-only preflight scanning.
- Exact duplicate detection by SHA-256.
- Conservative near-duplicate candidate grouping in v0.1.
- Human-reviewable reports.

The project must not claim:

- Guaranteed low platform duplicate score.
- Guaranteed approval.
- Existing adoption unless verified.
- Legal originality or copyright safety.
