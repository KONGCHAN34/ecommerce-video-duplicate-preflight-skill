# Platform Risk Disclaimer

This project is a local preflight tool. It does not guarantee that any e-commerce, short-video, advertising, marketplace, or social platform will approve, reject, rank, downrank, flag, or classify a video in any specific way.

The scanner reports local signals such as file hashes and conservative metadata similarity. Platform systems may use additional signals that this project does not see, including upload history, account behavior, captions, thumbnails, audio, visual fingerprints, product context, policy rules, and reviewer decisions.

Use this project to reduce avoidable review workload and prioritize human review. Do not use it to evade platform rules.
